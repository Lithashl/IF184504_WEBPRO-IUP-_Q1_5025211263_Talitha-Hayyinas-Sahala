Hometown

Surabaya is a vibrant city located in East Java, Indonesia. It is the second-largest city in Indonesia after Jakarta and serves as a commercial and industrial hub for the region. Surabaya has a rich history as a major trading port and played an important role in the struggle for Indonesian independence. The city's economy is driven by manufacturing, trade, and services, with a focus on textiles, electronics, and automotive industries. Surabaya is also a transportation hub, with a large seaport and airport, making it a gateway to other parts of Indonesia. Overall, Surabaya is a bustling city with a rich history and culture, offering visitors a unique glimpse into the heart of Indonesia.
